Growing in relationship03 / 06
The gap
I was good at talking. I was bad at listening.
I thought being heard meant the other person agreed with me, but those two things have nothing to do with each other.
You can win every argument and still lose the relationship.
@drawoheriter
4 — Content
Growing in relationship04 / 06
What actually matters
The most useful skill I learned was how to repair, not how to avoid conflict.
How to come back from a hard conversation without making things worse than before you started.
Healthy relationships are not conflict-free. They are repair-capable.
@drawoheriter
5 — Content
Growing in relationship05 / 06
The long game
Growing together means both people change, not just one.
If only one person is evolving, you eventually end up in different places without realising it happened.
The question is not are we compatible now. It is are we growing in the same direction.
@drawoheriter
