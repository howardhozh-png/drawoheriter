## Rotation (starting Day 22)

As of 2026-07-15, content rotates through 4 topics in a strict repeating cycle instead of running
21 straight days on one theme before switching. Order: **navigating career → building in public →
managing finances → learning about life**, then repeat. `generate.mjs` reads whichever `Overline
label:` immediately precedes each `### Day N` heading, so every day below carries its own label —
that's what drives the rotation, not the old per-theme blocks.

The "Fitness and discipline" theme (previously Days 43–56) is retired and replaced by "Learning
about life" — broader than fitness, and matches what Howard actually wants to cover.

Days 22, 26, 30, 34 (building in public) and 23, 27, 31, 35 (managing finances) are reused from the
original Theme 2 / Theme 4 drafts below, renumbered into the new cycle. Four days were dropped as
duplicates of posts already shipped outside this plan: the original "zero customers" and "unsexy
market" days (already posted as `day1-mockup` and `bip1-unsexy-market`), and the original MCP-tools
and first-month-mistakes days (already posted as `vibe-coding-mcp-tools` and `vibe-coding-mistakes`).

Days 25, 29, 33 (navigating career) and 24, 28, 32, 36 (learning about life) are freshly written —
career's original 21-day arc is fully used up through Day 21, and life had zero drafts before this.
Both pools run out again after Day 36; before generation reaches Day 37, add more real angles for
those two topics (the building-in-public and managing-finances pools have more runway — see the
draft pool at the bottom of this file).
