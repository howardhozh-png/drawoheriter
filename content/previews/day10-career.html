The alumni network is built for corporate problems, not startup problems
Corporate problems: navigating org politics, landing a role, getting a reference.
Startup problems: finding your first 10 customers, pricing a product nobody has heard of.
Different game. The playbook does not transfer the way everyone implies it does.
